Great White Shark Basics:

  • COMMON NAME: Great white shark
  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: Carcharodon carcharias
  • TYPE: Fish
  • DIET: Carnivore
  • GROUP NAME: School, shoal
  • AVERAGE LIFE SPAN: 70+ years
  • SIZE: Up to 21 feet long
  • WEIGHT: Up to 4,500 pounds
